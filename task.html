3 Favourite places in IIT Kharagpur

Main Gate

  • The gate of the dream which was completed after 2 years of struggle.
  • Having the best photograph of my life.

Main Building

  • Heart of the IIT Kharagpur campus.

  • Having some of the best places like Bhatnagar Auditorium, Raman Auditorium and Maggu Room.

Vikramshila Foyer

  • A place with the most beautiful lights at night time.
  • A place located in the Ramanujan Complex.
  • Having some of the internal events here in the the mesmerising cold breeze.
